Spring Training Conference - A New Bloom

Hello CNH Circle K!


Spring Training Conference (STC) is an annual district event for the upcoming leaders of CNH! At this special district event, you will have the opportunity to learn important leadership skills, meet other passionate Circle K members, and start the new term with your newly found knowledge of Circle K and how to lead your club! This event will consist of various workshops, Divisional Council Meetings, and an exciting service project. Come out and meet new friends from all over the district!
