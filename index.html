Spring Training Conference - A New Bloom

Hello CNH Circle K!


Spring Training Conference (STC) is an annual district event for the upcoming leaders of CNH! At this special district event, you will have the opportunity to learn important leadership skills, meet other passionate Circle K members, and start the new term with your newly found knowledge of Circle K and how to lead your club! This event will consist of various workshops, Divisional Council Meetings, and an exciting service project. Come out and meet new friends from all over the district!

4/1/2021

Hey CNH!

It’s Spring and you know what that means. It’s time for the first district event of the new term! The flowers are sprouting and it’s time for Spring Training Conference (STC) 2021: A New Bloom!

What is STC? STC is an annual district event for the future leaders of CNH! This year, the event is 100% virtual and 100% FREE to attend! From May 8-9, you will have the opportunity to learn important leadership skills, meet other passionate Circle K members, and start your new term with your newly found knowledge of Circle K and how to become a better leader! From various workshops, Divisional Council Meetings, a service project, and networking event, STC 2021 will be jam-packed with activities that will make you feel like you're blooming with your Circle K friends and family! So what are you waiting for? Register for the event through your home club today! 🌸💐🌸
